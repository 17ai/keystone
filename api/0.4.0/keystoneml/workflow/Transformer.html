Transformer

abstract class Transformer[A, B] extends TransformerOperator with Chainable[A, B]

Transformers are operators that may be applied both to single input items and to RDDs of input items. They may be chained together, along with Estimators and LabelEstimators, to produce complex pipelines.

Transformer extends Pipeline, meaning that its publicly exposed methods for transforming data and chaining are implemented there.

A

input item type the transformer takes

B
